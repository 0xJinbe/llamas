What is the Llamaverse?
Llamaverse is a club and community for NFT investors. We are determined to provide our holders with both valuable utility and education to help them thrive in the NFT space as it evolves. We also intend to connect to other communities to help bring some of our benefits to other projects in the space.
What is the supply of the Llamaverse?
The supply consists of 500 Animated 1:1 Llamas and 3500 Static Llamas. Each Llama comes with a set of benefits and each generate different amounts of $SPIT.You can learn more about it here: Llamaverse medium
What Kind of Utility Does Llamaverse Have?
The utility revolves around owning the NFT and generating $spit. You will be able to use $SPIT generated from your Llama in MANY ways and we will continue to find utility to add to $SPIT. To summarize the utility, you will be able to use $spit to claim massive whitelist opportunities, enter ETH raffles, redeen for L2 items from other ecosystems, generate $SPITBuddies which is our cross breeding implementation and so much more. Owning the NFT will also give you access to tools, RooTroop's Bounty System, P2E Ecosystems, Our NFTWorld which you can play to earn for ETH/WL Spots, Access to our exclusive discord and so much more.
Will I Be Able to Sell $Spit or Just Use It for Utility?
We will be providing so much utility to this token that you won’t want to even sell it. Some of the utilities include: whitelist opportunities, raffles, customization of llamas, P2E Items and BREEDING (with utility).Theoretically, you should be able to make money off generation $SPIT by using this utility. We do not want $SPIT to be tradable. We will not be providing liquidity to $SPIT as we want this to be used internally. Our goal is to make it valuable with internal use.
